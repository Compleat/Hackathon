About us

Compleat is dedicated towards helping people with dietary restrictions find restaurants. More than just a search website, find restaurants that are specifically catered towards your dietary needs.

Our Leaf Rating

Our in-house speciality Leaf Rating provides you with a indication of how many dishes at each restaurant fit the dietary restriction you're looking for. Now you can find more than just a soup or a salad!
